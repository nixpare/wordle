Welcome to Nix Wordle!

This is an unofficial version of the game Wordle but with a new word to guess every time the page is reloaded!

The goal of the game is to guess the secret word in at most 6 attempts

Instructions

If you are using a touch device its enough to click the grid and the keyboard will show up, then type 5 letters (you can delete if you make a mistake) and press Enter in order to check the row

ATTENTION: the chosen word as your try must exist, otherwise it will not be approved and you will delete the row and retry. The only exception is the last attempt, so be careful to not get it wrong!

Rules

Fill the 5 cells, then press Enter. The control animation will start:

W

If the letter is grey it means that it's not present in the word to guess

W

If the letter is yellow it means that it is present in the secret word but not in the right position

W

If the letter is green it means that the letter is already in the right place!
